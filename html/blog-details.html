List of Countries without Coronavirus case

While COVID-19 has impacted nearly every corner of the globe, there have been reports of countries that have managed to keep the virus at bay, at least to some extent. These countries often have implemented strict border controls, quarantine measures, and effective public health interventions. Places like Palau, Nauru, Tuvalu, and some smaller island nations in the Pacific have reported minimal to no cases of COVID-19. Additionally, several African nations such as Lesotho, Comoros, and São Tomé and Príncipe have reported relatively low case numbers. However, it's important to note that limited testing capacity and underreporting could contribute to the perception of these countries being COVID-free. Vigilance and ongoing public health efforts remain crucial in the global fight against the pandemic
